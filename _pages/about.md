### About
I'm Ziyan Jiang and I'm currently working as an applied scientist at Amazon, Alexa AI. I obtained the M.S. degree at Johns Hopkins University (JHU) and B.S. degree at Nanjing University (NJU). My research is primarily focused on Natural Language Processing, Information Retrieval and Speech Recognition. My current research focuses on 1)developing personalized natural language understanding systems, 2)integrating factual knowledge into models and systems, 3)exploring the capabilities and applications of large language models (LLMs).

I am open to any collaboration opportunities or just a coffee chat. If you are interested in speaking with me, feel free to send me an email. (ziyanjiang528@gmail.com)
